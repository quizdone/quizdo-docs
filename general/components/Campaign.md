# Campaign component

## Overview

A **Campaign** is a **sequence of exams** with **conditions** to move learners to the next step—used for longer flows than a single quiz.

> Campaigns build on **Quizzes/Exams** and **Questions**; categorization and **[Tags](Tags.md)** apply when selecting content for each exam.

## Purpose

- **Multi-exam flows**: Ordered or conditional progression.
- **Progress**: Track state across exams in the campaign.
- **Conditions**: Rules for advancing (scores, completion, time windows—product-specific).
